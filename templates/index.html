Purpose

This app gives a quick AI screening to identify whether an uploaded eye image shows signs of cataract or looks normal. Five trained models (DeepCNN, DeepANN, ResNet, VGG, AlexNet) vote for a consensus-based answer, ensuring reliable results.

⚕️

Medical Disclaimer

Intended as a screening aid — not a medical diagnosis. If the system flags a potential cataract, please consult a qualified ophthalmologist.

How to Use

Get a cataract analysis in under 30 seconds.

📋 Step-by-step Guide

1

Upload an Eye Image

Click the upload zone — or drag & drop. Accepted: JPG, PNG, WEBP. Use a well-lit, clear photo of a single open eye.

2

Start Vision Analysis

Tap ✦ Start Vision Analysis. All 5 CNN models run in parallel and the AI report generates — usually under 10 seconds.

3

Read Your Results

Verdict + confidence, model votes, 4 clinical indicators, 3-tier explanation (Simple / Technical / AI), and a full Llama-3 medical report.

4

Chat with Medical AI

Tap Medical AI (bottom-right) to ask questions. Switch reply language — English, Telugu, or Hindi.

❓ FAQ

Is this a medical diagnosis?▾
No. This is a screening aid — not a substitute for a qualified ophthalmologist. If cataract is detected, book an appointment with an eye specialist.
What makes a good photo?▾
Use a well-lit, in-focus photo of one open eye. Avoid heavy glare, dark images, or photos with the eye partially closed. A clear smartphone photo is sufficient.
How accurate is the system?▾
Five CNN models (DeepCNN, DeepANN, ResNet, VGG, AlexNet) vote via ensemble — reducing individual model bias. Accuracy depends on image quality. Always treat results as a first-pass screen.
What does the 3-tier explanation show?▾
Simple: plain language for anyone. Technical: ophthalmology-level feature analysis. AI / Model: how the CNN pipeline detected cataract from pixel patterns and feature maps.
Can I use any eye photo?▾
Upload one eye at a time. Works with standard smartphone photos — no fundus camera or slit-lamp required.
Is my data stored?▾
Images are processed in memory and not permanently stored. Your results exist only in your current session.
⚕️

Always see a doctor

If you experience sudden vision changes, blurriness, or halos — do not rely on this tool. Seek immediate professional medical attention.
