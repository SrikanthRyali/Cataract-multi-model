✦ About

This app gives a quick AI screening to identify whether an uploaded eye image shows signs of cataract or looks normal. Multiple trained models vote for a consensus-based answer.

Intended as a screening aid — not a medical diagnosis. If the system flags a potential cataract, please consult an eye specialist.

⚡ Tech Stack

Backend: Python · Flask · PyTorch. Frontend: Tailwind CSS with JS. Utilities: OpenCV for validation, Pillow for image handling, Groq LLM (Llama-3 70B) for summaries and chat.

❓ Help

Upload: click here to browserand upload a image of one open single eye. Images showing unrelated objects are rejected.

Chat: click the "Medical AI" button at the bottom-right corner to open the assistant panel. Switch reply language from the dropdown in the chat header.
